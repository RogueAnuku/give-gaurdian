Give Guardian

Providing peace of mind to those who support charitable causes. Give Guardian is a web browser extension designed to verify nonprofit organizations.

The case study details: by: Anika McPhee Dhwani Trivedi Chiara Fallani Desiree Henry
Problem:

The current philanthropic market is struggling with user engagement and proof of legitimacy. Leading to a diminished sense of donor confidence and a decline in contributions.

The solution:

With Give Guardian, we believe that by creating a user-centric, transparent, and intuitive charity donation platform for individual donors, we will achieve a stronger sense of security, trust, engagement, and meaningful impact on their philanthropic endeavors, while also ensuring that the intentions behind every act of giving are genuinely met.
