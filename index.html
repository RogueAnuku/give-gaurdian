Give Guardian

Granting reassurance to individuals who champion charitable endeavors, Give Guardian is a browser extension meticulously crafted to authenticate nonprofit organizations.

The team: Anika McPhee (User Research), Dhwani Trivedi (Coding), Chiara Fallani (User Interface), Desiree Henry (User Interface)

The motive behind charitable giving is noble, yet certain individuals exploit the kindness of others. As a donor, it is important to know for certain the legitimacy of the organizations that you choose to support.

Enter our web-browser extension concept, Give Guardian, designed to easily verify Not-for-profit organizations

Design Decisions

Our goal is to simplify the process of contributing to unfamiliar causes. To achieve this, we added a 'compare feature' in our product. This feature will provide users with recommendations for similar nonprofit organizations (NPOs) to the one they are considering supporting. By offering these recommendations, we aim to eliminate the need for users to go through multiple steps to find a cause to contribute to."

We've strategically emphasized the 'compare' and 'search' features by incorporating two prominent call-to-action buttons on the vertical side of the application. To enhance visibility, we've introduced a contrasting pop of color, ensuring that these key actions stand out for users.
