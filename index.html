Give Guardian

In the course of our assignment, we envisioned a groundbreaking web-browser extension tailored to empower philanthropists, varify charities, and organizations alike. Leveraging cutting-edge AI authentication, we offer a streamlined process for these entities to secure verified accounts, fostering trust and authenticity.

The team: Anika McPhee (User Research), Dhwani Trivedi (Coding), Chiara Fallani (User Interface), Desiree Henry (User Interface)

The motive behind charitable giving is noble, yet certain individuals exploit the kindness of others. As a donor, it is important to know for certain the legitimacy of the organizations that you choose to support.

Enter our web-browser extension concept, Give Guardian, designed to easily verify Not-for-profit organizations

Case Study Overview

Empathy

One of the techniques we deployed to better understand the users was the creation of our user persona

Our target demographic focuses on adults aged 61-71, as secondary research indicates that this age group is the most inclined to contribute.

What people had to say about assessing the legitimacy of a not-for-profit:

Interview Participant

"100% trust is so important. Anything money-related matters. Anytime I see that there's an *, I pay attention. For me, a positive experience is how you can make me feel and then guide me through the process easily. Don't rush me through."

Interview Participant

"I don't know if I trust it (artificial intelligence) yet. I think if it comes from an organization like Google, I would trust it. They already have all my account information! I won't trust ChatGPT if they ask me to enter my personal information".

Interview Participant

"I'm not familiar with the market to tell what the gaps are. I think giving options to donate food or clothes instead of only financial donations. Not sure if sustainable but offering different types of donation would fill up a gap."

Interview Participant

I would like to see something like a blue tick which can assure me of the legitimacy. Also if possible I would like to see a brief score on how the charity is donating the money it receives".
