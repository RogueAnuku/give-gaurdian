Give Guardian

Providing peace of mind to those who support charitable causes. Give Guardian is a web browser extension designed to verify nonprofit organizations.

The team: Anika McPhee (User Research), Dhwani Trivedi (Coding), Chiara Fallani (User Interface), Desiree Henry (User Interface)
Problem:

The philanthropic market is presently encountering hurdles in fostering user engagement and providing proof of authenticity. As a result, donor confidence has waned, leading to a decline in contributions. According to users, the length of time spent on task was a major pain point and a priority feature in our feature maxrix

The solution:

With Give Guardian, our vision is to establish a user-centric, transparent, and intuitive charity donation verification application tailored to individual donors. Through this approach, we aim to instill a heightened sense of security, trust, and engagement in philanthropic endeavors. Our goal is to ensure that every act of giving truly fulfills its intended purpose and creates a meaningful impact and minimize the time spent on task.

Design Decisions

Our goal is to simplify the process of contributing to unfamiliar causes. To achieve this, we added a 'compare feature' in our product. This feature will provide users with recommendations for similar nonprofit organizations (NPOs) to the one they are considering supporting. By offering these recommendations, we aim to eliminate the need for users to go through multiple steps to find a cause to contribute to."

We've strategically emphasized the 'compare' and 'search' features by incorporating two prominent call-to-action buttons on the vertical side of the application. To enhance visibility, we've introduced a contrasting pop of color, ensuring that these key actions stand out for users.
